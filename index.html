What is BathML?

Events

Monthly meetups, featuring talks, workshops and collaborative learning on a wide variety of data science and machine learning topics

Community

A friendly group of people who enjoy working together, sharing ideas and discussing the latest developments in machine learning

Bath Machine Learning Meetup was founded in October 2016, as a place for anyone interested in machine learning to meet like-minded people in Bath and the surrounding area. Since then, our community has grown to over 700 members in size, and we regularly welcome attendees from as far afield as Bristol, Oxford and Cardiff!

At heart, BathML is about learning and sharing knowledge – many of our events feature talks and workshops presented by members of our community. Please do get in touch if you have ideas!
